About Us

At an altitude of 3,500 ft, our beans are sourced from some of the most renowned plantations in Harar, Ethiopia & Chikmangalur and Coorg in India. Our proprietary formulations and crystallisation technology yields a bold and powerful coffee that is ready to help you seize the day. Rendezvous Coffee’s blend of supplements sourced from plant extracts and natural compounds have been scientifically formulated to work synergistically with your coffee. Our coffee is made using 100% Arabica beans.
